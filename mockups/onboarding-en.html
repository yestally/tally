This is the onboarding prototype for Tally — a task delegation and tracking system for human–AI teams. Unlike Slack/Teams, you don't see unread messages; you see decisions to make and work your agents completed.

Three questions:

  1. 5 seconds in — can you tell what this product does?
  2. Which 1–2 things excite you most vs your current IM? Which confuse or put you off?
  3. If you used this daily, what would worry you most?
